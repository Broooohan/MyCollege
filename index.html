About the Institute

Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kota (IIIT Kota) is one of the 20 IIITs being set up by the Central Government in Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode.

IIIT Kota currently offers all the amenities, academic and non-academic to its students that can help them flourish and serve the nation with all their apprehension in the various fields of technology. The admission is made through central counselling of candidates who qualify in JEE (Mains).
